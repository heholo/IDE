Motivation for our choices

The challenge in this visualization was how to represent the spouses in a tree structure. We accomplished this by making the edges connecting spouses different from the edges connecting parents and children. The legend was made to explain this choice to the viewer.

We also made certain to signify the importance of the family patriarch Donald Trump through a variety of visual elements: a large portrait with a golden border and a larger and more fancy font.
